## Overview

This work explored consensus models, transaction throughput, and the energy footprint of large-scale financial systems. I focused on translating network-level assumptions into measurable cost models.

### Research Focus

We compared proof-of-work and proof-of-stake models, estimating infrastructure costs at national scale.
