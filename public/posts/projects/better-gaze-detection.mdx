## Overview

We studied perception-driven models for gaze estimation, focusing on how humans interpret eye contact on 2D displays. My work centered on simulation setups and quantitative evaluation.

### Experimental Setup

The project included controlled user studies and synthetic data generation to model gaze alignment errors.
