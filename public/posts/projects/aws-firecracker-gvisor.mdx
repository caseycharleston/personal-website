## Overview

We benchmarked cold starts, memory overhead, and isolation guarantees across representative serverless workloads. The work focused on setting up reproducible harnesses and documenting key tradeoffs for real-world deployments.

### Environment Notes

The harness compared warm and cold start paths, captured median and tail latencies, and recorded resource utilization under mixed workloads.

### Next Steps

Future work includes automating more real-world request traces and scaling the experiment to larger function catalogs.
