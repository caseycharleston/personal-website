---
title: Cotypist is moving to a subscription model
date: 05/22/2026
---

Cotypist, an app I've used for some time now, is going on a subscription-based model. I've been trying to cut down on subscriptions and unfortunately will need to stop using it. I'd happily pay for a lifetime license, but I understand that the developer needs a consistent source of income.

I will say that it is a little absurd that an app powered by local LLMs needs a subscription to run, since no API tokens are being eaten by the app itself.
